Outfitted

Outfitted is a participatory design project aimed at creating a mobile app for showcasing outfits, tagging items with prices, and fostering community engagement. This platform bridges self-expression and discovery, enabling users to connect through fashion.

Key Features

  • Outfit Showcase: Upload and display your favorite outfits.
  • Interactive Tagging: Tag items with prices and store details.
  • Community Engagement: Like, comment, and share outfit inspirations.
  • Profile Customization: Personalize your profile to reflect your style.

Design Process

Our design process was rooted in participatory design methods, ensuring the app met user needs. The process included:

  1. Conducting surveys and interviews to gather user insights.
  2. Developing user personas to represent diverse user needs.
  3. Performing competitor analysis to identify gaps in the market.
  4. Iterative prototyping and testing to refine app functionality and design.
